About us

Our mission

Faith in action, hope for every child. Blessed Hands Ministries exists to share the love of Jesus Christ by caring for vulnerable children, strengthening families, and serving our community with compassion. Through faith, love, education, prayer, and practical support, we strive to restore hope, transform lives, and remind every child that they are deeply loved, valued, and never forgotten.

Our vision

A future where every child is loved, every family has hope, and every life reflects God's love. We envision a world where no child is left behind because of poverty, hunger, or hardship. Our dream is to raise generations of children who grow in faith, receive education, discover their God-given purpose, and become leaders who bring positive change to their communities. Together, with God's guidance and the kindness of people around the world, we believe every helping hand can change a life forever.

About us

My name is Rotich Hillary, and I am the founder of this ministry. On February 20, 2024, God placed a burden on my heart that I could no longer ignore. I looked into the eyes of children who had been forgotten—children who lacked food, love, education, and hope. I knew I couldn't change the whole world, but I believed I could change the world for at least one child. Our ministry began with just 7 children. We had very little, but we had faith, compassion, and a heart determined to serve. Every meal shared, every smile restored, and every prayer lifted reminded us that love can accomplish what money alone cannot. Today, by God's grace, our family has grown to 40 children. Behind every child is a story of pain, loss, and struggle—but also a story of hope. Every child deserves to feel loved, protected, and valued. Every child deserves the chance to dream again. Yet, there are still so many children waiting. Every day we meet young lives crying out for help, but our resources are limited. It breaks our hearts to know that we cannot reach every child who needs us. Our dream is not to stop at 40 children. We are praying and believing that one day we will reach hundreds of children, providing them with food, education, clothing, medical care, and, most importantly, the love and hope they deserve. To everyone who supports this mission, you are not simply making a donation—you are becoming part of a child's story. You are helping turn tears into smiles, hunger into hope, loneliness into love, and fear into a brighter future. Together, we can remind every child that they are not forgotten, that they are deeply loved, and that their future is worth fighting for. One act of kindness can change a life. One caring heart can change a generation.
